# Vertical Image Update Guide

This guide explains how to update the vertical (category) images in the Westend Corporation website.

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Open your web browser and navigate to: http://157.173.221.140/admin
2. Log in with your admin credentials.

## Updating Vertical Images

1. In the admin dashboard, find the "Verticals" section and click on it.
2. You'll see a list of all verticals (categories) with their images.
3. Click on the vertical whose image you want to update.
4. In the edit form, you'll see that most fields are read-only, but the "Image" field can be edited.
5. Click "Choose File" to select a new image from your computer.
6. After selecting the image, click "Save" at the bottom of the page.

## Image Requirements

For best results, follow these guidelines for vertical images:

- **Dimensions**: Recommended size is 800x600 pixels
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or SVG
- **File size**: Keep under 2MB for optimal performance
- **Content**: Use high-quality images that represent the category well
- **Style**: Use images with good contrast that will work well with text overlays

## After Updating

After updating an image, you may need to clear your browser cache to see the changes on the website. Press Ctrl+F5 (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to perform a hard refresh.

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues while updating images, please contact the website administrator for assistance.
